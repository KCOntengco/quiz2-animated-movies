Inside Out



Eleven-year-old Riley's life is turned upside down when her family moves from Minnesota to San Francisco. Inside Riley's head, her emotions - Joy, Sadness, Anger, Disgust, and Fear - try to navigate the new city and keep Riley happy. However, when Joy and Sadness get lost in the labyrinth of Riley's mind, the other emotions struggle to keep her happy.
